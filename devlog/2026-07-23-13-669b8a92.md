**Devlog Entry: Synthetic Identity Fraud in Machine Identities**

**Date:** 23 July 2026

**Summary:** A recent cybersecurity update highlights the emerging threat of synthetic identity fraud in machine identities, where attackers create fake digital identities to breach IoT devices, APIs, and cloud services. This type of fraud leverages AI-generated credentials, stolen personal data, and social engineering tactics to evade detection. As the attack surface expands, it's essential to stay vigilant and implement robust identity verification and access control measures to mitigate this threat.
