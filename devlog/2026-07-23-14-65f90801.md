# Security Update: Google Adds Selfie Video Recovery for Users Locked Out of Their Accounts

_2026-07-23 14:12 UTC_

[Source](https://thehackernews.com/2026/07/google-adds-selfie-video-recovery-for.html)

**Devlog Entry: Google Account Recovery Update**

Date: 23 July 2026

**Summary:** Google has introduced a new account recovery feature that allows users to verify their identity using a selfie video. This update raises concerns about the potential for deepfake attacks, where malicious actors can create convincing but fake video content to bypass security measures. As a result, developers should be aware of the importance of implementing robust video verification and deepfake detection techniques to maintain account security.
