**Summary:**

Chaos ransomware has been utilizing msaRAT to route C2 (Command and Control) traffic through headless instances of Chrome and Edge browsers. This tactic allows attackers to evade traditional security measures and maintain persistence on compromised systems. The use of browser-based C2 channels highlights the evolving nature of ransomware threats and the importance of staying vigilant in detecting and responding to emerging attack vectors.
