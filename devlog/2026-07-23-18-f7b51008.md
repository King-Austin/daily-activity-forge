# Security Update: Russian Espionage Group Exploited Zimbra Zero-Day to Steal Mail and 2FA Codes

_2026-07-23 18:53 UTC_

[Source](https://thehackernews.com/2026/07/russian-espionage-group-exploited.html)

**Devlog Entry: Zimbra Zero-Day Vulnerability**

**Date:** 23 July 2026

**Summary:**
A zero-day vulnerability in Zimbra email servers has been exploited by a Russian espionage group to steal email contents and 2FA codes. This vulnerability highlights the need for timely patching and monitoring of email servers to prevent such attacks. The incident serves as a reminder to prioritize security updates and implement additional security measures to protect sensitive user information.
