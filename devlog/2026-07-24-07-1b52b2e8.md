### Fake Notepad++ Plugin Delivers MATCHBOIL.V2 in UAC-0099 Attacks

A newly discovered fake Notepad++ plugin has been found to deliver the MATCHBOIL.V2 malware in UAC-0099 attacks. This plugin, designed to appear legitimate, exploits users' trust to install malicious software, emphasizing the importance of verifying plugin sources and enabling strict security settings. Developers should prioritize validating plugin authenticity and staying informed about emerging threats to maintain the security of their applications.
