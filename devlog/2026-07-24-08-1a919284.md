**Summary:**

Recently, NodeBB patched eight critical vulnerabilities discovered by AI-powered security tools, which could allow unauthorized access to administrator accounts and private chat messages. These flaws highlight the growing importance of AI-driven security testing in identifying and mitigating complex software vulnerabilities. This incident serves as a reminder to prioritize regular security audits and updates in our own projects.
