# TIL: Writing by Hand is Good for your Brain

_2026-07-23 15:23 UTC_

[Original Source](https://nealstephenson.substack.com/p/writing-by-hand-is-good-for-your)

**TIL:** Handwriting can have a cognitive benefit by stimulating neural connections and improving memory retention. This has implications for UI/UX design in digital tools, where we may need to reconsider the role of handwriting and manual input in user interfaces. For example, incorporating handwriting recognition or manual note-taking features in apps could potentially enhance user understanding and recall, rather than solely relying on keyboard input or touchscreen interfaces.
