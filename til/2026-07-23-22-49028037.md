# TIL: Namecheap Gave My Account to an Unverified Third Party Just Because They Asked

_2026-07-23 22:09 UTC_

[Original Source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=49028037)

### TIL: Importance of Verification and Authorization Controls

TIL that a lack of robust verification and authorization controls can lead to serious security vulnerabilities. The recent incident involving Namecheap handing over an account to an unverified third party highlights the importance of implementing proper access controls and verification mechanisms. This incident serves as a reminder to developers to ensure that their systems validate and authenticate user requests before acting on them, and that access is strictly limited to authorized parties. I'll be reviewing our current implementation of OAuth and authorization flows to ensure we're not exposing ourselves to similar risks.
