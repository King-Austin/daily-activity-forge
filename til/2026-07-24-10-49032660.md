# TIL: It's getting harder to focus every day

_2026-07-24 10:25 UTC_

[Original Source](https://glyphack.com/attention/)

### TIL: Attention Economy and Software Development

TIL that our brains are struggling to focus due to the constant stream of information from the internet, social media, and other digital distractions, as highlighted by Glyphack's "Attention" article on Hacker News. As a software developer, this has significant implications: our users' attention spans are decreasing, and their ability to engage with complex tasks is waning. I'm reminded to consider the design of our applications and services, incorporating features that promote focus and minimize distractions, such as reducing notifications, implementing Pomodoro timers, and optimizing UI for minimal cognitive load. This could be an opportunity to innovate and create more user-friendly experiences that cater to the changing needs of our audience.
