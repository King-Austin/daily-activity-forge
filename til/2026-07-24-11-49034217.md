# TIL: The case for MUDs in modern times

_2026-07-24 11:55 UTC_

[Original Source](https://www.andrewzigler.com/feed/the-case-for-muds-in-modern-times)

**TIL**: I came across the article "The case for MUDs in modern times" by Andrew Zigler and was reminded of the potential for text-based, interactive systems in modern software development. The author's argument that the principles of MUDs (Multi-User Dungeons) can be applied to modern problems, such as creating complex, dynamic, and immersive user experiences, has implications for the design of APIs, command-line interfaces, and even game development. Specifically, I learned that a modular, data-driven approach to building interactive systems, as seen in classic MUDs, can provide a scalable and maintainable solution for complex, user-facing applications.
