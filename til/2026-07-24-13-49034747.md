## TIL: The Power of Em Dashes in Code

While reading the article "Em dashes are fucking amazing" by psychotechnology, I learned about the versatility and nuance that em dashes can bring to coding and technical writing. Em dashes (—) can be used to indicate a break in thought or a pause in a sentence, providing a clear distinction between clauses without the need for cumbersome parenthetical phrases or semicolons. This can significantly improve code readability, especially when dealing with complex conditional statements or asynchronous functions, allowing developers to concisely convey intent and reduce cognitive load.
