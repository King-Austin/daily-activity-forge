# TIL: Patreon laying off 20% of staff

_2026-07-24 13:40 UTC_

[Original Source](https://www.patreon.com/jackconte/posts/painful-update-164628951)

**Today I Learned (TIL)**

TIL that companies facing financial strain often prioritize cost-cutting measures, such as mass layoffs, which can be a double-edged sword for software development. On one hand, reduced staff can lead to increased technical debt due to the loss of knowledge and expertise. On the other hand, it may prompt teams to adopt more efficient processes, automation, and cloud-native strategies to compensate for reduced resources. In the case of Patreon, a platform built on WebSockets and WebRTC for real-time communication, a significant reduction in staff may lead to a re-evaluation of their infrastructure and architecture, potentially driving innovation in areas like serverless computing, containerization, or low-latency data processing.
